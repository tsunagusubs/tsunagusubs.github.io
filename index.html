- policy -

Violate these rules and you will feel my wrath. *roars*

  • This is more of a personal thing, but I don't translate NSFW works. Whether a song is NSFW or not is at my discretion.
  • Do not publish any translation on this site that does not appear on my channel on any video sharing site - they're meant to be viewed as text. I'm saving you a copyright strike.
  • You may use my translations for derivative works (wiki and personal sites are OK) as long as you credit me. There's been drama over this with Hazuki no Yume and Rachie, so I can't stress this enough: Translation takes hard work and we deserve proper attribution. Also, NO VIDEO REPRINTS!
  • Don't use my translations on covers that are not your own unless you can show me explicit permission from the utaite or others involved in production.
  • Commercial use of my translations is prohibited. Ask the producer for permission.
  • The three bullets above also apply to my original PV works (Reku Mochizuki's "Summer Ghost")
  • If you're still unsure, email me (see below).
