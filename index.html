About Me

My name on the interwebs is 193姫, Japanese number play for "Ikusahime" and I run the TsunaguSubs channel. I have been an avid fan of Vocaloid music and other types of J-Music for five years and have recently decided to undertake the challenge of translating. I am freelance and by no means professional, but I am on the Vocaloid Wiki's Recommended Translators list. This is my third year of enrollment in a Japanese course and I am looking forward to expanding my knowledge while bringing you fresh works. よろしくお願いします！ (P.S. I have a noticable bias for EDM and ballads)

Request and Usage Policies

[Please read this before contacting me. Otherwise if you have any other questions by all means do so.]

  1. Do not reprint my videos on YouTube. Other video sharing sites hosted outside the US (such as the original video on NicoNico) are acceptable as long as you credit the producer and me, and refrain from monetizing them.
  2. I do not condone uploading my translations marked copyrighted on any video sharing sites.
  3. Posting my text translations/romaji lyrics on your website/blog/wiki, etc. and using these for your personal projects is fine with me as long as you give proper credit to the producer and me.
  4. I take most requests. Exceptions include R18+, gory, and "Questionable" works as well as songs from producers I marked as "beyond my Japanese level". If you ask for an album-exclusive/anisong I will be more than happy to post it as a text translation here. I may take a while as I ask for permission from most producers first.
  5. I am a high school student enrolled in various accelerated classes and involved in four extracurriculars. Please be patient as I may take 2-4 weeks to complete your request.
